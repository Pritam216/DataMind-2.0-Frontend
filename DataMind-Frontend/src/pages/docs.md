# DataMind Documentation

## Getting Started

Welcome to DataMind! This guide will help you get started with our data analysis and AI/ML platform.

### What is DataMind?

DataMind is a comprehensive platform designed to help you explore, analyze, and visualize your data with the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

## Features

- **Data Upload & Processing**: Easily upload and process your datasets
- **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**: Automatic analysis and summarization of your data
- **AI Chat Assistant**: Interactive chat interface for data insights
- **Visualization**: Beautiful charts and graphs for data exploration

## How to Use

### 1. Upload Data

Navigate to the home page and upload your dataset. We support CSV, JSON, and Excel formats.

### 2. Explore Your Data

Use the EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis) summary to understand the structure and characteristics of your dataset.

### 3. Chat with AI

Ask questions about your data using our AI chat assistant. Get instant insights and analysis.
